# Genesis Academy Strategy & Operations School 2.0 // Case Task

On my 15th attempt, I finally made it to the **Case Task** stage of Genesis Academy's **Strategy & Operations School 2.0**.

This repository contains my case solution for **TaskFlow**, a subscription-based task management app that showed a broad decline across downloads, MAU, paying users, and MRR. The goal of the case was to diagnose the decline, evaluate the market and competitors, develop strategic options, and recommend a clear 12-month direction.

## What This Case Covers

The case focuses on:
- problem diagnosis using product and business metrics
- market and niche analysis within subscription-based productivity tools
- competitor benchmarking
- strategic option assessment
- a recommended turnaround strategy with roadmap and KPI targets
- risk assessment and mitigation logic
